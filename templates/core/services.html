Frequently Asked Questions

How long does international shipping take?

Depending on the service level chosen, international shipping can take anywhere from 24 hours (Overnight) to 7 business days (Standard). Customs clearance in individual countries can sometimes add 1-2 days.

Can I change the delivery address after booking?

Yes, as long as the parcel has not left the origin sorting facility. You can update the delivery address via our portal or by contacting customer support immediately.

What happens if my parcel is lost?

While loss is extremely rare (less than 0.01%), all shipments are insured. We will initiate a tracking investigation, and if the parcel cannot be located within 5 days, we will process your insurance claim immediately.
